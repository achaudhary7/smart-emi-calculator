When Should You Transfer Your Home Loan?

Balance transfer makes sense when the interest savings exceed the transfer costs. Consider these factors:

  • Rate Difference: At least 0.5% lower rate is needed for meaningful savings
  • Remaining Tenure: More remaining years = more savings potential
  • Outstanding Amount: Higher loan = higher absolute savings
  • Transfer Costs: Processing fees, legal charges, stamp duty

What Are The Charges For Balance Transfer?

  • Processing Fee: 0.25% to 0.5% of loan amount (negotiable)

  • Stamp Duty: Varies by state (0.1% to 0.5%)

Documents Required for Balance Transfer

  • Existing loan account statement
  • Property documents (original)
  • NOC / Foreclosure letter from current bank
  • Income proof (latest 3-6 months salary slips)
  • Bank statements (6-12 months)
  • KYC documents
